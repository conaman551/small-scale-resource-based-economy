Objective:


Build an archetypal, small-scale resource based community / town, with optimised self-reliance, factoring in housing, food, education, goods production, energy, recreation and personal growth.


Base parameters. 


Capacity: 150 people, a realistic size given limited money and resources


Architecture: Circular and Hexagonal,  most efficient shapes for a settlement


Area: 200 acres minimum. 


External Economy: Monetary


Internal Economy: Resource Based


Construction Method: Phased, 6 segments equal in area will make up the entire town, only one segment needs to be built at a time because each segment is self-sufficient.


Energy: Solar and Wind
